About the lawyer

  • In 1998, graduated from Yaroslav Mudryi National Law University of Ukraine, graduated from Law faculty with a degree in jurisprudence and was qualified as a lawyer
  • From 1998 to 2010 worked in the prosecutor's offices of Kharkiv region in the area of pressing federal charges in the courts
  • In 2009, graduated from the National Technical University "Kharkiv Polytechnic Institute", got a complete higher education in the specialty "Pedagogy of Higher School" and was qualified as a teacher in universities and higher educational establishments
  • On June 10, 2010, passed a qualification examination and gained the right to practice law
  • Since November 2011 Managing Director of the OOO Kharkiv Law Company
  • From September 1, 2013 was accepted to a teacher position in Kharkiv National Automobile and Highway University
